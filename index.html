Our Services

Comprehensive Eye Care

From routine eye exams to complex surgical procedures, we offer a full spectrum of ophthalmology services.

🔭

Comprehensive Eye Exam

Full vision assessment including refraction, eye pressure testing and retinal examination.

⚡

Cataract Surgery

Advanced cataract removal with affordable surgery packages and quick recovery.

✨

LASIK & Refractive

Laser vision correction for myopia, hyperopia and astigmatism.

👁️

Retina Treatment

Diagnosis and treatment of retinal disorders including diabetic retinopathy and macular degeneration.

🔵

Glaucoma Management

Early detection and management of glaucoma to protect your optic nerve and vision.

🌊

Dry Eye Clinic

Specialized treatment for dry eye syndrome using latest therapies and lubricants.

🔀

Squint Surgery

Surgical correction of strabismus (squint) for children and adults.

🩺

Corneal Ulcer Care

Expert diagnosis and treatment of corneal infections and ulcers.

👓

Contact Lens Prescription

Fitting and prescription of contact lenses with detailed follow-up care.
